# Roadmap

This document outlines the planned evolution of **Newsbit AI**.

The roadmap is intentionally incremental. Each phase builds upon the previous one while maintaining a simple user experience centered around a single AI chat interface.

---

# Phase 1 — AI News Summaries (Current Version)

### Goal

Help users understand today's most important news in under 10 minutes.

### Features

- User authentication
- AI chat interface
- Single supported prompt: **"Top 10 news today"**
- Fetch latest news from NewsAPI
- Generate AI summaries
- Display source name and article link
- Store articles and summaries in the database

---

# Phase 2 — Personalized News

### Goal

Deliver news that is more relevant to each user.

### Planned Features

- Categorize news articles
- Store article categories
- Allow users to select interests
- Personalized news recommendations
- Improved ranking of news based on user preferences

---

# Phase 3 — Deep AI News Analysis

### Goal

Move beyond summaries and help users understand the full context of important stories.

### Planned Features

- Detailed article explanations
- Pros and cons analysis
- Background and historical context
- Important facts and key takeaways
- AI-powered reasoning for complex topics
- Agent-based workflows for deeper analysis

---

# Phase 4 — Conversational AI Experience

### Goal

Transform Newsbit AI into a complete conversational news assistant.

Instead of creating separate sections for different features, the application will continue using a **single AI chat interface** where users can naturally interact with the system.

### Planned Features

- Ask questions about news articles
- Explain articles in simple language
- Compare multiple news stories
- Discuss ongoing events
- Context-aware conversations
- Multiple AI capabilities accessible through one chat experience

---

# Long-Term Vision

Newsbit AI aims to become an intelligent news assistant that helps users:

- Stay informed with minimal time
- Receive personalized news recommendations
- Understand complex topics through AI
- Interact naturally using a single conversational interface
- Explore news with intelligent agent capabilities

Every new feature should strengthen this vision while keeping the product focused, intuitive, and easy to use.
