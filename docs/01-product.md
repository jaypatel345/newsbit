# Newsbit AI

## Goal

Newsbit AI helps users understand today's most important news in just a few minutes through a simple AI-first experience.

Instead of browsing multiple news websites, users interact with a single AI input, receive the Top 10 news stories of the day, and quickly understand each article through concise AI-generated summaries.

---

## Target Users

Newsbit AI is designed for people who want to stay informed but have limited time, including:

- Busy professionals who have less than 10 minutes a day to catch up on the latest news.
- Students who want to stay updated with current affairs without reading lengthy articles.
- Software engineers and other tech professionals who want a quick overview of important news during their workday.
- Anyone looking for a fast, reliable way to understand the day's top stories.

---

## Problem

Reading news from multiple websites is time-consuming. Most articles contain unnecessary details, making it difficult for people with busy schedules to stay informed.

Users often:

- Spend too much time reading articles.
- Visit multiple news websites to compare coverage.
- Miss important news because they don't have enough time.

---

## Solution

Newsbit AI simplifies news consumption by:

- Providing a single AI input bar on the home page.
- Running the predefined prompt **"Top 10 news today"**.
- Fetching the latest news from trusted news providers.
- Generating concise AI-powered summaries for each article.
- Displaying every article with its category, source, publication date, and link to the original article.

---

## Version 1 Scope

The first version of Newsbit AI will include:

- Complete user authentication (Sign Up, Login, Forgot Password, JWT Authentication).
- A single AI input bar on the home page.
- Support for one predefined prompt: **"Top 10 news today"**.
- Fetch today's top news from trusted news providers.
- Generate AI summaries for each article.
- Display each article with its category, source, publication date, featured image (if available), and original article link.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Newsbit AI aims to evolve from a simple AI-powered news summarizer into a personalized AI news assistant. Future versions will understand each user's interests, provide personalized news recommendations, support natural language conversations, and help users stay informed through intelligent, context-aware news experiences.
